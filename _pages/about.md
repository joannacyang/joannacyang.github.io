Before that, I obtained my BEng from [Beihang University (BUAA)](https://www.buaa.edu.cn/) and MSc from [National University of Singapore (NUS)](https://nus.edu.sg/)

My research interests include (Multi-Agent) Reinforcement Learning/ Decision Making, Large Language Models (LLMs), and AI in Fintech.
